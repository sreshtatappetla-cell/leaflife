🌿 Storage Guide for Leafy Vegetables

🥬 Fresh Leaves (70% - 100%)

  • Store in refrigerator immediately
  • Wrap leaves in paper towel to absorb moisture
  • Keep inside airtight container
  • Do not wash before storing
  • Keep in vegetable crisper drawer
  • Avoid storing near fruits like banana and apple
  • Ideal temperature: 1°C – 4°C

🍃 Moderately Fresh Leaves (40% - 70%)

  • Wash and dry completely before storing
  • Remove yellow or damaged leaves
  • Store in zip-lock bag with small holes
  • Use within 2–3 days
  • Keep in fridge but not in freezer
  • Cook instead of eating raw if needed

🥀 Low Freshness / Spoiling Leaves (Below 40%)

  • Use immediately
  • Best for cooking (soups, curries)
  • Do not store for long
  • Remove rotten parts before use
  • Boil and refrigerate for short-term use
  • Compost if unusable

❄️ General Tips

  • Always keep leaves dry
  • Moisture causes faster rotting
  • Use paper towel method
  • Do not tightly pack leaves
  • Air circulation increases shelf life
  • Check daily and remove spoiled leaves
