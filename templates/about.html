🌿 About Leafy Vegetables

Leafy vegetables are rich in essential nutrients, vitamins, and antioxidants. They help boost immunity, improve digestion, and support overall health. Including leafy greens in your diet can significantly improve your well-being.

🌟 Fun Facts

  • Spinach was made famous by Popeye for strength.
  • Lettuce was first cultivated in ancient Egypt.
  • Kale contains more Vitamin C than oranges.
  • Leafy greens naturally detoxify the body.
  • Some greens like arugula have a spicy taste.

💚 Health Benefits

  • Rich in fiber for better digestion
  • High in vitamins A, C, and K
  • Supports heart health
  • Helps in weight management
  • Contains antioxidants that fight diseases
